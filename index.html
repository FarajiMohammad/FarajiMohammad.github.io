A Bit About Me

Welcome! I am Mohammad Faraji, a researcher in Machine Learning with interests in representation learning, feature selection, optimization, and multi-label learning. My research explores how mathematical modelling, matrix factorisation, and low-rank representations can be used to develop more effective and interpretable learning algorithms. I enjoy studying the fundamental principles behind machine learning methods and contributing to the development of novel approaches for complex learning problems.
